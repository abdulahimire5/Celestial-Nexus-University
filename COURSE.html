Graduate Programs

The center for graduate studies (JCGS) of Celestial Nexus University is a postgraduate and higher learning center established by Celestial Nexus University to provide quality postgraduate research-oriented programs that further contribute to the community. JCGS strives to be at the forefront of national graduate and research advancements, founded on global standards and integration of research knowledge, and aspires to cultivate an environment that promotes both intellectual and social exchange among the students and faculty members with the ultimate goal of producing research of the highest quality, thus significantly contributing to the global knowledge creation and dissemination.
